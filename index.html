The Jarvis Model

One AI agent.
Multiple protection layers.

Jarvis is being built as a unified autonomous defense layer for the modern business. Instead of treating website security, email security, identity risk, and infrastructure signals as disconnected problems, Jarvis is designed to understand the full picture and act intelligently.

// MODULE_01 — WEB

Website Defense

Detect malicious traffic, attack patterns, exploit attempts, bots, and suspicious requests in real time.

// MODULE_02 — EMAIL

Email Guard

Identify phishing, dangerous attachments, spoofing attempts, and malicious inbound messages before they cause damage.

// MODULE_03 — IDENTITY

Identity Watch

Monitor suspicious login behavior, unusual access attempts, and risk signals around company identities.

// MODULE_04 — RESPONSE

Autonomous Response

Block, quarantine, escalate, or report high-confidence threats automatically — with plain-English clarity.
